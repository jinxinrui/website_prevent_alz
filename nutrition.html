Nutrition Suggestion

Foods to eat and avoid

Recommended Food

  • Turmeric

    Turmeric called super spice is a powerful anti-inflammatory and many studies show that regular consumption will prevent the onset of getting Alzheimer's and boosts cognitive ability.

  • Coconut oil

    Coconut oil is free of cholesterol and trans fats. Studies show that coconut oil may prevent dementias like Alzheimer's. There is nothing wrong with trying it.

  • Extra virgin Olive oil

    Research shows that extra virgin olive oil may protect the brain from getting Alzheimer's.

  • Carrots

    Carrots contain beta-carotene that prevents Alzheimer's. Eat carrots that have vivid color, it contains high beta-carotene.

  • Walnuts

    A brain shaped nut is really good for the brain, loaded with antioxidants, omega 3 fatty acids when regularly consumed improves motor coordination, memory, brain activity and reduces the chance of getting Alzheimer's.

  • Blueberries

    Blueberries are superfruits fully loaded with powerful antioxidants that repairs cells, DNA damaged by free radicals. Research suggests that they help to prevent, reduce or slow down the chance of getting Alzheimer's.

  • Lemon balm

    Lemon balm, a herb that consumed increases the cognitive functions. Research also shows that lemon balm worked significantly well on Alzheimer's patients and boosted their cognitive ability.

  • Ashwagandha

    Ashwagandha mixed with warm water or honey has a great effect on the mental system. Ashwagandha is a brain booster. It alters the chemical activity, boosts cognitive ability and also helps Alzheimer's patients by inhibiting the formation of beta-amyloid plaques.

  • Salmon

    Salmon is a great source for Omega 3 fatty acids and are considered as a great food for the brain.

  • Pumpkin seeds

    Contains vitamin E, folate and choline that helps with brain cognition, consuming pumpkin also helps and is also a great food for Alzheimer's.

Not Recommended Food

  • Processed Cheese

    These foods build up proteins in the body that have been associated with Alzheimer’s.

  • Processed Meats

    Processed meats, such as bacon, smoked turkey. smoked meats like these contain nitrosamines, which cause the liver to produce fats that are toxic to the brain.

  • Beer

    Most beers contain nitrites, which have been linked to Alzheimer’s.

  • White Sugar

    Consuming these causes a spike in insulin production and sends toxins to the brain.

  • Popcorn

    Microwave popcorn contains diacetyl, a chemical that may increase amyloid plaques in the brain. Research has linked a buildup of amyloid plaques to Alzheimer’s disease.
