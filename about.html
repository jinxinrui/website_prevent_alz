About us

The Alzheimer’s Prevention project aims to raise the awareness on Alzheimer’s disease to people who are in their midlife, age between 40 to 60 years old. Studies show that chance of Alzheimer’s development is highest at 65 and death. Therefore, with considering decline on body fitness level after 40, staying physically and mentally active would be beneficial not only in preventing Alzheimer’s Disease but also increasing the fitness level.

Our website provides important information regarding to disease risk factors and providing comprehensive source of information that users can use and reach out to learn more.

All information, suggestions and calculation of this website are based on academic research. The references and related documents are provided under each web page.
